Birdcage

(Hangman lol)

Based on/inspired by hangman, but with birds instead!
This game will require you to guess a word related to birds (and it may or may not have hints)
and each letter you guess correctly, any bird that is in that letter's place will be set free!
But if you run out of guesses, then the bird(s) will be trapped forever! XP
